Ingredients
	*1 cup of black beans
	*1 cup of corn kernels
	*1/2 cup shredded cheese
	*1/2 cup of jarred salsa
	*4 whole wheat tortillas
	*oil
	
1. Toss together beans, corn, cheese, and salsa and spoon onto the tortillas, fold in half, and brush lightly with oil on both sides.

2. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 10-15 minutes, flipping over half-way through the baking time.
